Training and placement cell


The Training and Placement Cell of C E Cherthala is a student coordinated body which works under the principal and the placement officers. It functions as a launching platform for qualified candidates in realizing their dreams. TPC invited a wide range of organizations to the campus and offer services to the students in making intelligent and well-informed career choices. The office functions exclusively for facilitating students placements in various organizations before they pass out from the college.



Computer science engineering

The mission of the Computer Engineering Department of CE Cherthala is to generate high caliber computer engineers to meet the global demands. The Computer Engineering Department of CEC offers premier professional training with an eye on the modern development in Computer Science offering a 4-year B.Tech degree course in Computer Science and Engineering. The department owns versatile and powerful computing facilities. The curriculum is harmonically tuned with today's hardware and software industry.
